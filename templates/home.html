Fishing Light Boat Tracking

FLBTrack is a vessel light tracking application to find fishing potential zones located in the WPP 573 area. Let's start tracking potential fish zones In addition to tracking the ship's lights, you can also find out about the diversity of fish species in the ship area.

WPP 573

Fisheries management area for fishing, fish farming, conservation, fisheries research and development which includes inland waters, archipelagic waters, territorial sea, additional zones and Indonesia's exclusive economic zone.

Fish Diversity

WPPNRI 573 is potentially an attractive habitat for economically important fish such as skipjack, bigeye tuna, albacore tuna, madidihang, tuna, mackerel and lemuru.
