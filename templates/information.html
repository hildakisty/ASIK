FISH DISTRIBUTION

WPPNRI 573 is potentially an attractive habitat for economically important fish such as skipjack, bigeye tuna, albacore tuna, madidihang, tuna, mackerel and lemuru.

Swallowfish

Layang is the name of a type of fish belonging to the genus Decapterus, family Carangidae.Small pelagic fish caught in the wpp 573 area.

Skipjack

Skipjack (Katsuwonus pelamis) is a medium-sized fish of the Scombridae (tuna) family. The life pattern of skipjack is influenced by oceanographic factors, such as salinity, surface currents, and dissolved oxygen, which affect the seasonal migration period.

Tuna

Tuna (Euthynnus affinis) is one of the large pelagic fish species that has considerable reproductive potential. Oceanographic parameters such as SPL, salinity, and chlorophyll-a concentration can affect the presence and catch of tuna in marine waters.

Lemuru

Lemuru (Sardinella lemuru) is a small pelagic fish species found in the Bali Strait, East Java and Bali waters.
